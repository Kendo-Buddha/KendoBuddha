Biography




Liang Hung was graduated from National Hsinchu Senior High School. He is currently a senior student at National Taiwan University majors in Mechanical Engineering. Liang has a great interest in mechanism analysis and design, especially in spatial over-constraint mechanisms. He has an ongoing undergraduate research project on mechanical advantage analysis of spatial PSRC mechanism in the Mechanism Design Laboratory supervised by Prof. Kuan-Lun Hsu. Liang Hung is also interset in vehical dynamics. That is the reson why he joined the NTU Racing Team and participate in another ongoing undergraduate research project on race car vehical dynamic modeling in the Dynamic System Design Lab supervised by Prof. Wei-Jiun Su.
